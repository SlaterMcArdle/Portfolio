About Me

I'm a physicist turned engineer turned traveller. I started my career in robotics, building and programming various automation systems. Programming challenges ranged from microcontroller programming in C++ and Python to fully fleshed out desktop-based .NET automation programs. I also dabbled in web-based remote web apps for long distance automation control. After two and a half years in the field I got itchy feet and decided to hitch my way around the world by boat. Three years and five boats later I'd made it halfway around the world. Then the pandemic hit and sent me home. It's been a few years, but I've decided to pursue my passion for programming in the form ov web development. Find examples of my work below!
